About Us

Providing Distributed Data

We specialize in building and maintaining planetary-scale distributed data services.

Connecting Global Communities

We respect the legal rights, privacy, and cultural norms of our users around the world.

Engaging with Open Source

We believe in the power of open source to inspire, democratize, and bolster our software.

We’re a Distributed Data Company

We are building products and services with state-of-the-art distributed systems technology.

Strongly Consistent

Built on Alia, a new protocol for geographically-replicated distributed consensus that ensures data consistency and durability across cloud and edge services.

Legally Compliant

User privacy is a fundamental right. Security is our core design principal. We encrypt by default and allow you to control and understand where your application data goes.

Fast and Flexible

Hierarchical consensus is fast and flexible, which is essential to next-generation applications built with 5G, AI/ML, IoT, AR/VR, and whatever comes next!

Easy to Use

We give developers access to strong consistency at a higher level of the application stack, adding flexibility and visibility to your existing managed data services.

Drawing Strength from Scale

Supporting geo-distributed companies, creators, and communities in the modern digital landscape.

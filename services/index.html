AI Enablement Services

AI and LLMs offer powerful opportunities for organizations to leverage their domain expertise and data to automate workflows, personalize experiences, and deepen competitive moats, provided you make the right choices to maximize ROI, safety, and alignment.

Open LLM Integration

Connect your organization's workflows to open source or cloud service provider LLM APIs (e.g. OpenAI). Rotational creates custom plugins for your services to work seamlessly with provider LLMs.

Time Estimate:

3 - 6 months

Domain-Specific LLMs

Create a domain-specific LLM using open source models, your corpus, and transfer learning. We provide MLOps to deploy the LLM in your organization's infrastructure or can host for you.

Time Estimate:

3 - 6 months

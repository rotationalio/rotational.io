Chris Okuda is a backend software engineer with a decade of experience developing software in Python, Go, JavaScript, SQL, and many other languages and technologies. His expertise has been in big data ingestion, transformation, and processing, and in developing complex algorithms alongside industry experts. He holds a degree in Computer Science from the US Naval Academy, and a mathematics degree from the University of Hawaii. In his spare time, he enjoys woodworking and sim-racing classic cars.
